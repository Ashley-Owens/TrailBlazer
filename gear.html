Mt. Baldy Devil's Backbone Trail

Gear Recommendations

Weather Conditions:    76°   4 mph

Gear
Light-weight jacket shell - protection from wind gusts
Hat / Cap - protect face from sunburn
Sunglasses - protect eyes from sunlight

Terrain:  2,350 ft    Rocky

Gear
Hiking Shoes - ankle-supporting, rugged shoes
Hiking Pants - versatile for outdoor conditions
Small First Aid Kit - in case of emergencies

Estimated Time:   3.3 miles   4 hours

Gear
2 Liters of Water - trail is long, prevent dehydration
Trail Mix - standard burn is 1400 cal. Bring adequate food
Sun Block - long sun exposure can be harmful to the skin
Phone Charger - scenic photo spots and no charging points
